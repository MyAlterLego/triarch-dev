## Decisions Made

1. **Active tab styling** — `text-teal-300` + `border-b-2 border-teal-400` for active; `text-zinc-400 + border-transparent` for inactive (with `hover:text-zinc-200`). Saturated teal-cyan was chosen because StatusPill's `approved` family already uses that hue for the customer surface (CONTEXT.md UX-02 palette continuity). Underline-only — not a filled pill (UX-01 D-02 explicit).
2. **Layout duplicates auth check with child pages** — intentional and documented in a 12-line comment block at the top of `layout.tsx`. The layout is the customer-perceived 404 boundary; child pages still need their own `getCurrentUserContext` call to derive `userRole`/`currentUserEmail` props they pass to client islands. Two cheap DB lookups per request, single TCP connection — acceptable for separation of concerns.
3. **Removed CustomerHeader from 7 pages, not just the 3 main surfaces** — the plan's `read_first` step on Task 3 instructed verification of sub-pages, and grep confirmed all 4 sub-pages also rendered CustomerHeader. Cleaning all 7 in the same commit avoided a half-cleaned state where, e.g., `bugs/[id]` would render the header twice (once from layout, once from itself).
4. **`'use client'` source-text test (T1.8)** — adopted from Phase 18's `no-sub-claim` pattern; readFileSync + first-non-empty-non-comment line check. Guards against the catastrophic mistake of nesting a server-only component (whose `usePathname` would silently not work) inside a client tree.
